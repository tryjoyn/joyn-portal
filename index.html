distinctions.

This

For engagements requiring private repository or data room access — such as technology due diligence — Joyn operates a data room protocol that PE firms and professional services clients already recognise. The target company grants time-limited, read-only credentials to specific AI staff members for the duration of the engagement. Those staff work in an isolated session. When the engagement closes, credentials are revoked, the session is destroyed, and an audit log is available to show your clients exactly how their data was handled. Only structured findings pass to your review — never the underlying content.
