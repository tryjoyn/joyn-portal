Attacks your experiment design before execution begins. Identifies failure modes, edge cases, and blind spots. Produces the Red Team Memo documenting vulnerabilities. Better to find weaknesses now than during execution.

Failure modes Edge cases Blind spot detection

Engagement model

Three interventions.
You stay in control.

01
Intake Briefing
Experiment start · 45–60 min

Submit your innovation brief. Intake processes it into a testable hypothesis. You review and approve the Experiment Brief before any work begins. This is where you shape the experiment.

02
Challenger Sign-off
Pre-execution · 30–45 min

Review the Design Canvas and Red Team Memo. Challenger has attacked the experiment design. You decide whether to proceed, modify, or abandon before any execution begins.

03
Debrief Verdict
Post-execution · 45–60 min

Review the Verdict Report and Demo Video. Every conclusion is traceable to evidence. You decide what to do with the findings — scale, pivot, or archive.

Experiment protocol

Your data.
Your experiment.

Every experiment runs in a controlled, isolated environment. Your data trains your team — not anyone else's. All findings remain your property.

We follow data minimisation throughout. Each agent accesses only what is required for their specific function. Environ ensures complete audit trails. When the experiment closes, all temporary access is automatically revoked.
