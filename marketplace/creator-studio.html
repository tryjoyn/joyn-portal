Three creator tracks

Which creator are you?

Track 01

Domain Expert

Practitioners with deep field experience

You have 5+ years in your field and a clear point of view on how work in your domain should be done. You do not need to write code. The Joyn team handles everything technical — you bring the expertise.

Track 02

Domain-Technical Hybrid

Practitioners who also build

You combine domain expertise with an ability to contribute to the build process — workflow design, prompt architecture, or output review. Higher earning potential and faster time to market.

Track 03

Emerging Creator

Earlier-career practitioners with a specialisation

You are building deep expertise in a defined area and have a clear point of view, even if your career history is shorter. Supported pathway with a more structured build process.
