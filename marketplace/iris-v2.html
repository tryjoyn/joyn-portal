What Iris Does

Three core tasks. Done right.

◉

Monitors regulatory sources

Continuously scans state DOI websites, RSS feeds, and bulletin databases across all 50 states. Checks every 2 hours so you're never behind.

◉

Filters for relevance

Uses your lines of business and carrier preferences to determine which bulletins actually affect you. No noise, just signal.

◉

Alerts with context

When something material changes, Iris sends a plain-English summary explaining what happened, why it matters, and what action (if any) is needed.
