Engagement model

Three modes for
three moments in a deal.

01
Rapid Screen
Pre-LOI · 24–48 hours

Full eight-agent analysis on public signals only. Stack, team, security posture, repo health — all from open sources. Enough to make an informed bid or walk away before spending weeks on full diligence.

02
Full Diligence
Post-LOI · 48–72 hours

Repository access, data room documents, management interviews fed into the pipeline. IC-ready memo produced with deal risk register and prioritised DD action items. Your clients receive your brand throughout.

03
Portfolio Monitoring
Post-acquisition · Ongoing

Quarterly technical health reviews across your portfolio companies. Flags deterioration before it becomes a value leakage event. The same team that assessed the asset monitors its execution.

Data room protocol

Your data.
Never shared.

Every credential is scoped, read-only, and automatically revoked when the engagement closes. Your engagement data trains your team — not anyone else's.

We follow the data minimisation principle throughout. The team accesses only what is required for the specific domain being assessed — Jordan sees the repository, Riley sees the security configuration, they don't cross-pollinate access.

Scoped access — each agent accesses only their domain
Read-only — no write access ever granted
Auto-revoked — all credentials revoked at engagement close
Client-private — your data improves your team only
Audit trail — full log of what was accessed, when, by whom
